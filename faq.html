KYBG FAQs

What does KYBG stand for?

KYBG stands for 'Kiss Your Bugs Good-Bye!'

Are the pesticides toxic to pets?

No, the chemicals in the pesticides are not lethal to pets. But with routine precautions, remove your pet's toys away from areas of treatment prior to your appointment.

How long does the appointment take?

It does depend as some pests are harder to deal with than others, some treatments may only need 30 minutes versus more severe cases up to 8 hours.
